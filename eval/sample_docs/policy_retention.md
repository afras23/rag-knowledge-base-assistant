# Records Retention Policy

## Retention

Financial records must be retained for seven years after the fiscal year closes. Human resources personnel files are retained for five years after employment ends unless local law requires longer. Email archives for project delivery are retained for three years.

## Disposal

Secure disposal requires cross-cut shredding for paper and cryptographic wipe for electronic media. Disposal events must be logged with date, custodian, and volume.

## Legal hold

When litigation is reasonably anticipated, records subject to hold must not be destroyed until counsel releases the hold.
