# Employee Onboarding Handbook

## First week

New hires complete security awareness training and acknowledge the code of conduct. Equipment is provisioned through the standard catalog; laptops ship with disk encryption enabled by default.

## Benefits enrollment

Benefits elections occur within thirty days of hire. The company contributes to health premiums according to the published schedule in the annual guide.

## Performance

Probationary reviews occur at ninety days with documented goals aligned to the role profile.
